Welcome To Empire Heights Legends!

This blog attempts to explore the rich lore and intricate details of the former members of Empire Heights!

Empire Heights Legends is dedicated to preserving the stories and histories of the individuals who once played pivotal roles in the Empire Heights community. Through detailed articles, interviews, and archival research, we aim to shed light on the contributions and legacies of these former members. Whether you're a long-time resident or a newcomer interested in the area's past, our blog offers a wealth of information and insights into the lives that shaped Empire Heights. We compile stories off all types of people, both good and bad. For the only thing more dangerous than evil acts, is forgetting that they happened.Join us as we open all our eyes and delve into the fascinating narratives that continue to influence the present-day community.

From the great Cal Carrow, scourge of the net and father of all Fiends, to the infamous gang leader Johnny "The Blade" Marconi, whose reign of terror left an indelible mark on the streets of Empire Heights. From the suave and great founder of our city Mark Vandergotch, to the man who shot him dead in a duel. Discover the tales of redemption, betrayal, and heroism that define the legacy of Empire Heights' former members. Our blog serves as a tribute to their lives, ensuring that their stories are remembered and celebrated for generations to come. And, of course, the tales of the dark and shodow filled past of the veiled streets.

Join us on this journey through time as we uncover the legends of Empire Heights, bringing to light the stories that have shaped our community and continue to inspire us today. Whether you're seeking to learn more about the past or simply looking for captivating narratives, Empire Heights Legends is your go-to source for all things related to the former members of this storied locale.
