The Legend of Cal Carrow

Cal Carrow, often referred to as the "Scourge of the Net" and the "Father of All Fiends," is a legendary figure in the annals of Empire Heights. Born into a world of technological marvels and digital frontiers, Carrow quickly rose to prominence as a master hacker and cybercriminal. His exploits in the virtual realm were unparalleled, earning him both fear and respect among his peers and adversaries alike. Responsible fore creation of fiends, including the Azazel package, the first commonly used slice tool.

Carrow is responsible for the development of the net as we know it, beinng the man who harrassed net tech to the point of developing HEX 1.0. Carrow dissapeared mysteriously in 2005, leaving behind a legacy that continues to influence the digital landscape of Empire Heights. His story is one of brilliance, rebellion, and the relentless pursuit of freedom in a world increasingly dominated by technology. His actions would influence the creation of mindseye, and other groups like them across the globe.
